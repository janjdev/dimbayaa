Our MISSION

We creatively, facilitate opportunities for healing, restorative processes and self-expression socially, economically and politically.

Dimbayaa is a Mandeng word meaning family, and as such, we seek to catalyze interdependency, resilience, and mobility of all people underscoring the African Diaspora.

We seek to imbue the communities we work in with the joy, grace, passion and strength that is inherent within our art and enrich lives.

WHAT WE DO

Our core works consist of three types of arts and education engagement to create networks of support for community and professionals.

CLASSES AND WORKSHOPS

  • Provide traditional dance, music, and holistic education

  • Create social support interventions in community settings

  • Foster the exchange of ideas and best practices with other experts

  • Engage underserved communities, expanding, relationships and extending the reach of cultural art

PERFORMACE LECTURES

  • Magnify the relationship between art and knowledge with respect to the history, language and culture of African and African Diasporic peoples

  • Facilitate access to art and cultural experiences

  • Enhance and broaden understanding of cultural constructs and human society

CONCERT BALLETS

  • Preset artistic and theatrical peices that analyze historical and modern concepts to inform and challenge audience perceptions

  • Present unique ideas, expressions, beliefs and values of African and African Diasporic societies that lead out, or draw from each of us

  • Present expert movement technique and articulation of the body to the community at large
